Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

1. Introduction

FundLink Technologies ("FundLink," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, which connects founders and investors through a privacy-first approach.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Professional background and company information
  • Investment preferences and thesis (for investors)
  • Startup details and pitch materials (for founders)

Usage Data

  • Platform interaction patterns
  • Connection requests and match preferences
  • Communication metadata (not content)

3. Asymmetric Visibility Model

FundLink operates on a unique asymmetric visibility model designed to protect founder privacy:

  • check_circle Founders control what information investors can see
  • check_circle Investors express interest without accessing full founder profiles
  • check_circle Full profile access requires explicit founder consent
  • check_circle All communications are encrypted end-to-end

4. Data Security

We implement military-grade asymmetric encryption to protect your data. Our security measures include:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Secure data storage with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With verified investors or founders as part of matching
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
